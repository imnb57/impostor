How it works

Three rounds of
beautiful paranoia.

A full game takes about five minutes. Learning it takes about thirty seconds.

STEP 01

Get your role

Everyone sees the same secret word — except the impostor, who only learns the category. Nobody knows who got what.

STEP 02

Describe & bluff

Take turns describing the word without ever saying it. Too vague and you look guilty. Too precise and you hand it to the impostor.

STEP 03

Vote them out

Argue, accuse, then vote in secret. Catch the impostor and the crew wins — miss, and they walk away laughing.

Two ways to play

One phone, or everyone's.

🎉

Pass & Play

Zero setup, zero internet. Add names, pass the phone around the circle, and everyone holds the screen to peek at their own role. Perfect for a table, a car, or a queue.

🌐

Online rooms

Host a room and share the code. Friends join from their own phones and everything — roles, timer, votes — stays in sync in real time.

Install in 60 seconds

Not on the Play Store yet.

So you install it directly — the same way you'd install any app from outside the store. Android will ask once for permission, and that's it.

Download impostor.apk
  1. Tap download on your phone

    Grab the APK straight from the GitHub release — always the newest build.

  2. Allow "install unknown apps"

    Android shows this prompt for anything outside the Play Store. Approve it for your browser and continue.

  3. Open and gather your friends
